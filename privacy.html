Privacy Policy — KerygmaCode
Effective Date: May 17, 2026

BibleBits does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Ever.

Overview

BibleBits ("the App") is developed and maintained by KerygmaCode. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of the App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Information We Do Not Collect

BibleBits does not collect any personal information from its users. Specifically, we do not collect:

— Names, email addresses, or contact information
— Location data
— Device identifiers or advertising IDs
— Usage analytics or behavioral data
— Financial or payment information
— Any other personally identifiable information

Data Stored on Your Device

BibleBits stores your daily streak count locally on your device using your device's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to KerygmaCode or any third party. You can clear this data at any time by deleting the app.

Notifications

BibleBits may request permission to send you local notifications to remind you to maintain your daily streak. These notifications are generated entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or data transmission. You can disable notifications at any time through your device's Settings app.

Third-Party Services

BibleBits does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The App operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external servers during normal use.

Children's Privacy

BibleBits is appropriate for users of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information, there are no special concerns regarding children's privacy. The App complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and revise the effective date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
