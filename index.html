Mission

Kerygma Code was founded with a simple mission: to build technology that helps people engage more deeply with their faith. BibleBits is our first step — a tool designed to make Scripture memorization simple, consistent, and accessible in everyday life. But it's only the beginning.

What's Coming

We're actively developing Trellis, a discipleship-focused network created to help believers grow together through connection, accountability, and shared spiritual habits.

Our Goal

Our goal is to create tools that don't just inform, but transform — tools that help people proclaim truth, build meaningful rhythms, and make a lasting impact.
